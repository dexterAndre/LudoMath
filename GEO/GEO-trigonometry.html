Intro to Trigonometry

Trigonometry is the study of triangles. At first it might seem straightforward, but there are so many uses for triangles in game development that this chapter will be pretty large. One of the biggest uses of trigonometry comes from the fact that most rendering engines operate on triangular meshes. In order to calculate lighting, collisions, and to measure distances and angles, we turn to the various tools that trigonometry gives us.

Solving Triangles

Game engines work with points. These points are usually located in a Cartesian coordinate system. The fact that our coordinate axes are perpendicular to each other means that we can put right triangles as a measurement tool wherever we'd like.
