Coordinate Systems

Coordinate systems can be used in all the chapters of LudoMath, so why does it go under the category "Linear Algebra"? My reasoning for this is that we work very intimately with coordinate systems in this chapter. We will travel between different coordinate systems, and even transform the coordinate systems themselves! Taking some time to get well acquainted with the various types of coordinate systems, and how we can manipulate them, will help us when we get to the "Transformations" section.
